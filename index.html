MCSS is a pure CSS library for displaying and formatting mathematical expressions.

Though heavy on markup, it allows for proper and easy formatting of almost any mathematical expression, from a simple 1 2 (vertically aligning within a p is still hard) to extremely complex expressions, such as 1 2 + 1 Π n=0 ∞ n 1 2 + Σ m=0 ∞ Π p=0 ∞ n m p m .
